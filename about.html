About FoodPicky

FoodPicky is a online food ordering platform for all the foodies in Nepal, where they can search for their favourite restaurants, get food delivered to their home or office, get awesome deals when they dine-in at the restaurants, read and post restaurant reviews, explore restaurant menu, earn credits to their FoodPickyWallet and much more.
